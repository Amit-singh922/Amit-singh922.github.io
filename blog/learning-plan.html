Learning Plan

Long Term Goal/Career Pathway:

My long-term goal is to become a skilled software developer with expertise in web development. My aim after this course is to create or improve existing software or webites my organisation is using.

Strengths and Limitations:

  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Adaptability to new languages
  • Effective communication and teamwork abilities
  • Tendency to get overwhelmed by large tasks
  • Occasional difficulty in maintaining focus for extended periods

Skills to Develop:

  • Time management and organization
  • Emotional intelligence and resilience
  • Leadership and conflict resolution

Workload Management:

To manage my workload effectively during the programme, I commit to:

  • Prioritizing tasks based on urgency and importance, using techniques such as Eisenhower's Urgent/Important Matrix.
  • Breaking down larger tasks into smaller, manageable chunks to avoid feeling overwhelmed.
  • Communicating openly and proactively with peers, facilitators, and industry/community representatives to set realistic expectations and negotiate adjustments when necessary.

Managing Productive and Safe Collaboration:

I will ensure productive and safe collaboration by:

  • Respecting the perspectives and boundaries of others, fostering an inclusive and supportive learning environment.
  • Actively listening to feedback and addressing concerns in a timely and respectful manner.
  • Being mindful of diversity and cultural differences, promoting empathy and understanding among all participants.

Managing Stress and Seeking Help:

When things start to feel overwhelming, I will:

  • Take short breaks to recharge and refocus, practicing mindfulness and relaxation techniques.
  • Reach out to my peers, facilitators, or support resources for guidance and encouragement.
  • Utilize the resources provided by Dev Academy, such as counseling services or additional workshops, to address any challenges that arise.

Expectations from the Facilitation Team:

  • Provide clear communication and guidance on programme expectations, deadlines, and learning objectives.
  • Offer constructive feedback and support to help me identify areas for improvement and develop my skills.
  • Foster a collaborative and inclusive learning environment where all participants feel valued and supported.

Scheduling Information:

I have commitments to my job and other personal activities, including dance classes and social gatherings. I will block out specific times in my schedule for programme-related activities and ensure that I allocate sufficient time for learning and assignments. Additionally, I will communicate any scheduling conflicts or changes promptly to ensure transparency and manage expectations effectively.
